Who Am I

Hi I'm David, a creative web developer who loves building dynamic websites and web applications. If your looking for a proficient React developer who works in fast-paced and collaborative environments, has an customer focused mindset, and loves learning new technologies, I think you just stumbled upon your next hire.

Before web development, most of my career has been in various sales roles including a car salesman for Mercedes-Benz and most recently a real estate agent. I also served a short term in the U.S Army as a field artillery tactical data systems specialists, which is a complicated way of saying I was part of a unit that provided fire support to infantry units by shooting big cannon rounds from a long distance. My job was to manage the advanced computer systems that established safe firing parameters and also set up communication for mobile units.

My first introduction to programming came along when I bought my wife a book on programming so that she can learn for work. I was curious one day and read a few pages. Before you know it I was hooked. At first it was just a hobby, but playing around turned into an strong desire to learn as much as possible. I completed a course called "Python For Everyone" and immediately knew that coding was for me. Being in sales so long, I knew first hand how much a great website or application could do for a product so I decided to pursue web development. Through hours and hours of project-based learning and online courses like The Odin Project and FreeCodeCamp, I managed to self-teach myself how to develop dynamic websites based around the Javascript language. Now I am looking for a front-end developer role based around React.
